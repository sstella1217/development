Class Schedules and Fees

BEGINNER

Bring your basic obedience skills and learn an exciting sport! This class is for those dogs that have not been introduced to agility obstacles and have the basic obedience skills of "come", "sit", "down", and "stay". Dogs must be at least six months old to participate. The class begins with the dog on leash. You and your dog will learn the different obstacles and begin the always challenging, at times hilarious journey, one obstacle at a time, in the sport of agility.
$75 per month, one hour per week -- Terms and Conditions - 1 (see below)
MONDAYS at 8:15 pm      TUESDAYS at 8:15 pm      WEDNESDAYS at 2:00 pm

INTERMEDIATE CLASS

Improve on your basic agility skills! This class is for those dogs that have had some basic training on agility obstacles. Dogs should be able to work off leash and respond to basic obedience commands of "come", "sit", "down", and "stay". Emphasis will be on improving obstacle performance, speed and accuracy and learning to perform obstacles in sequence.
$75 per month, one hour per week -- Terms and Conditions - 1
MONDAYS at 7 pm      TUESDAYS at 7 pm      WEDNESDAYS at 3:15 pm

ADVANCED/MASTERS CLASS

You have the skills, now let’s crank it up! This is an on-going class for those desiring to perform at a competitive level. Handler/dog may join class at anytime. The class is one hour per week. Emphasis will be on improving performance and handling. This will be accomplished through short exercises to introduce the handling moves and full courses working under distractions. One class per month will be dedicated to one of the agility games offered by the different agility organizations.
$75 per month, one hour per week -- Terms and Conditions - 1 and 2
MONDAYS at 5:30 pm      TUESDAYS at 5:30 pm      WEDNESDAYS at 6:30 pm

MASTERS/ELITE CLASS

Practice makes perfect!! This is an ongoing class for dogs that have earned an agility title. Or, for those who have the skills and just like to play! Continued emphasis will be placed on skills developed in the advanced class. Also, additional emphasis is put on improving response and control, obstacle discrimination, performance of obstacles at a distance, and directional commands.
$75 per month, one hour per week -- Terms and Conditions - 1 and 2
WEDNESDAYS at 6:30 pm

PRIVATE LESSONS

One-on-one sessions offer an excellent alternative when the handler desires more individual attention than can be devoted in a regular class, or when special training problems need to be addressed.
$40 per half-hour/$60 per hour. Call 903-876-3004 to schedule.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS - 1
A dog shall not exhibit signs of aggression toward people or other dogs at any time or for any reason. Aggressive dogs will not be permitted, and no refund of fees paid will be made if a dog is dismissed from classes due to aggressive behavior.
TERMS AND CONDITIONS - 2
Handler/dog may join class at any time. First month pro-rated. Payment first week of each month thereafter. If fewer than three classes in a month are held due to inclement weather, holidays or other causes, a pro-rated refund will be given. No refund will be made if class is held and handler/dog does not attend.
